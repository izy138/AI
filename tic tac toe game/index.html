Minimax

Minimax explores possible moves to evaluate outcomes and selects the move that maximizes the player's minimum guaranteed score. It's simple but can be slow for large trees.

Alpha-Beta

Alpha-Beta pruning reduces the number of nodes Minimax must evaluate by discarding branches that won't change the final decision, giving identical results faster.
